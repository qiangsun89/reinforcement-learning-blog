书籍推荐
************
计算机领域
=============

* R. S. Sutton and A. G. Barto, Reinforcement learning: An introduction (2nd Edition). MIT Press, 2018.(被誉为强化学习领域内的圣经)
* C. Szepesvari, Algorithms for reinforcement learning. Springer, 2010.

自动控制领域
================
* D. P. Bertsekas and J. N. Tsitsiklis, Neuro-dynamic programming. Athena Scientific,1996.
* Bertsekas D. Reinforcement learning and optimal control. Athena Scientific, 2019.
* Meyn S. Control systems and reinforcement learning. Cambridge University Press, 2022.

这几本书的数学性和专业性非常强
